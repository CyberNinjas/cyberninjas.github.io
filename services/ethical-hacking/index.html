Ethical Hacking

Ethical Hacking, also known as Application Penetration Testing, or Dynamic Application Security Testing (DAST) is when a trained security professional with permission from the organization attempts to hack the application.

Get StartedLearn More

At Cyber Ninjas all vulnerabilities found during the assessment are then fully documented in a final report which includes details about the vulnerability, evidence that the vulnerability exists, and remediation advice. At the conclusion of the engagement this report can then be reviewed with the organization in a conference call. During this call the findings will be explained further and any questions about the findings or the remediation advice can be discussed. At Cyber Ninjas we believe that “Security comes with knowledge”™, and it is our job to be sure you understand the vulnerabilities found and the risks associated with them so that you can make the appropriate business decisions.

At Cyber Ninjas we offer many levels of ethical hacks for various types of applications. Our standard offerings are outlined below, but customized options are also available. If you have any questions about these offerings or need assistance in picking the offering that is appropriate for your application, please feel free to contact us.

Web Application Assessments

Cyber Ninjas offers several different levels of Web Application Assessments ranging from purely automated scanning with false-positive removal to purely manual assessments. Our typical engagement is 1-2 weeks. Please contact us to determine what level of Web Application Assessment is best for your application and business requirements.

Mobile Assessments

Cyber Ninjas offers mobile assessments for iOS and Android applications. Mobile Assessments range from manual testing of the application and interfacing servers, to full reversing of the application including bypassing jailbreaking and root detection. Our typical engagement is 1-3 weeks. Please contact us to determine what level of mobile assessment is best for your application and business requirements.

Client/Server Assessments

Cyber Ninjas offers assessments of traditional thick client client/server applications. Client/Server engagements range from manual testing and light reversing of the application, to heavy reversing of the application and testing associated servers. Our typical engagement is 2-4 weeks. Please contact us to determine what level of mobile assessment is best for your application and business requirements.

Cyber Red Team

Cyber Ninjas offers Cyber Red Team engagements. Cyber Red Team engagements focus on the entire digital perimeter of your organization and are not isolated to a single application. The scope of a Cyber Red Team can vary drastically depending on the business need of the organization but may include compromising servers, social engineering, and brute forcing or cracking passwords. Typically the goal of a Cyber Red Team is to see how far you can get within the organization utilizing publicly available channels. This type of ethical hacking engagement most closely resembles what an actual hacker would attempt to do. Typical engagements range from 4-16 weeks. Please contact us to discuss more fully what you may or may not want in scope so we can help determine the appropriate length for this engagement.
