We believe that individuals learn best when there is a combination of lecture and hands-on instruction. As a result for all of our training classes we seek to have 30-50% of the time spent in labs. There is much that can be learned by hearing, but we should be doers also to help solidify the material and store it in our memory banks.

Onsite Security Training

Cyber Ninjas® offers a variety of classes for partial or full-day onsite security training. Topics include Ethical Hacking, OWASP Top 10, and general security awareness. We will also create and deliver custom material, per request. For more details on what classes are currently available, please contact us.

Computer Based Training

Cyber Ninjas® is currently working on building out a set of Computer Based Training (CBT) classes for mobile and web application security. These classes are self-paced and can be made available to an individual, or an entire team. For pricing, or other details, please contact us.

Cyber Mentoring

Cyber Ninjas® offers mentoring on various application security topics to assist individuals in growing their knowledge and security capabilities. Mentoring can be customized to the needs of the individual, but usually involves assessing the individuals knowledge, setting goals, and then customizing home-work and research assignments to grow their knowledge, answering questions where needed.
